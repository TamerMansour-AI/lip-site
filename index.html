The Problem

Why abstract science concepts fall flat.

Abstract ideas stay abstract when visuals are inconsistent and time is scarce.

  • Students and caregivers struggle when ideas stay abstract and text-heavy.
  • Inconsistent visuals across classes make it harder to build lasting understanding.
  • Staff time is scarce; translating standards into engaging media is slow.

The Solution

Purpose-built explainers with structured outputs.

  • Animated explainers that stay aligned to your provided sources and standards.
  • Clear, simple visuals that remove ambiguity for younger and multilingual learners.
  • Structured deliverables that are easy to review, share, and reuse across grades.

Included

Fixed curriculum packages (Years 1–5 Science)

CLEAR is delivered as complete, curriculum-aligned packages—built to standardise instruction across classrooms while keeping delivery simple for teachers.
  • Concept clips (60–90s) — Short visuals to introduce, clarify, or reinforce each concept.
  • Static recap visuals — One-page summaries for review, homework support, and parent-friendly clarity.
  • Teacher talking points — Simple delivery notes: what to say, what to emphasise, and common misconceptions.
  • Curriculum mapping — Clear alignment to learning objectives and lesson sequence for Years 1–5 Science.

FAQ

Quick answers for decision-makers.

Do you invent content?

No. Everything stays tied to the materials you provide—source-bound and citation-friendly.

How fast is a pilot?

Typical pilots land in 5–7 business days once sources and scope are locked.

How do we review visuals?

You get thumbnails, sample frames, and drafts before final animation.

Are captions included?

Yes—captions and readable on-screen text are standard. Audio description can be added on request.

How is data handled?

You control what’s shared. Source files stay within your preferred storage and review loop.
