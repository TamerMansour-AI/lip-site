## Problem
- Headline: Why abstract science concepts fall flat.
- Points:
  - Students and caregivers struggle when ideas stay abstract and text-heavy.
  - Inconsistent visuals across classes make it harder to build lasting understanding.
  - Staff time is scarce; translating standards into engaging media is slow.

## Solution
- Headline: Purpose-built explainers with structured outputs.
- Points:
  - Animated explainers that stay aligned to your provided sources and standards.
  - Clear, simple visuals that remove ambiguity for younger and multilingual learners.
  - Structured deliverables that are easy to review, share, and reuse across grades.

## What’s Included (Pilot)
- Headline: Low-risk pilot you can approve fast.
- Items:
  - 1 intro video plus 2–3 short concept clips.
  - Consistent narration and on-screen text for accessibility.
  - Optional background music direction (or stay silent-first).
  - Thumbnails and sample frames for stakeholder review.
  - Export formats for vertical and horizontal sharing (MP4 + image sets).
